Search Syntax

The collection search bar accepts Scryfall-style query syntax with additional keywords for collection-specific filtering. You can combine multiple keywords in a single query to narrow results.

For the full Scryfall syntax reference, see scryfall.com/docs/syntax. The sections below cover the supported keywords and our collection-specific extensions.

Collection-Specific Extensions

These keywords are unique to DeckDumpster and are not part of Scryfall's syntax. They filter based on your personal collection data.

Status

Filter cards by their collection status. The default view shows owned and ordered cards.

QueryDescription
status:owned Cards you currently own (the default filter includes owned + ordered)
status:ordered Cards that have been ordered but not yet received
status:sold Cards that have been sold
status:traded Cards that have been traded away

Date Added

Filter by the date a card was added to your collection. Uses ISO 8601 format (YYYY-MM-DD).

QueryDescription
added>=2024-01-01 Cards added on or after January 1, 2024
added<=2025-12-31 Cards added on or before December 31, 2025

Price

Filter by the card's current market price in USD.

QueryDescription
price>=5.00 Cards worth $5 or more
price<=1.00 Cards worth $1 or less

Deck and Binder Assignment

Filter cards based on their deck or binder assignment. Supports name matching and wildcards.

QueryDescription
deck:"Mono Red" Cards in the deck named "Mono Red"
deck:* Cards in any deck
deck!=* Cards not in any deck
binder:"Trade" Cards in the binder named "Trade"
binder:* Cards in any binder
binder!=* Cards not in any binder

Collection Flags

Shorthand flags for common collection queries.

QueryDescription
is:unassigned Cards not in any deck or binder
is:decked Cards in any deck (same as deck:*)
is:bindered Cards in any binder (same as binder:*)
is:wanted Cards on your unfulfilled wishlist

Sorting

Control the sort order of results. These can be combined with any other query.

QueryDescription
order:name Sort by card name
order:price Sort by current market price
order:added Sort by date added to collection
order:cmc Sort by mana value
order:rarity Sort by rarity
direction:asc Sort ascending (A-Z, low to high)
direction:desc Sort descending (Z-A, high to low)

Boolean Logic

Combine multiple conditions using boolean operators.

OperatorUsageExample
AND (implicit) Space-separated terms are ANDed together c:r t:creature — red creatures
OR Use or between terms c:r or c:g — red or green cards
NOT Prefix a term with - -c:b — not black
Grouping Use parentheses to group conditions (c:r or c:g) t:creature — red or green creatures
Negated group Prefix a group with - -(c:r or c:g) — neither red nor green

Operators

OperatorMeaning

Example Queries

t:creature c:r mv>=3 Red creatures with mana value 3 or higher
is:foil r:mythic Foil mythic rares in your collection
s:lci r>=rare Rares and mythics from Lost Caverns of Ixalan
o:draw -c:b Cards with "draw" in their text that are not black
deck:* is:foil All foil cards currently assigned to any deck
is:unassigned added>=2024-01-01 Recently added cards not yet assigned to a deck or binder
status:ordered Cards that have been ordered but not yet received
price>=10 order:price direction:desc Cards worth $10 or more, sorted by price descending
